Top 5 Places to Visit in Bangalore

Discover the Garden City's most iconic destinations

1. Lalbagh Botanical Garden

Famous for its glass house and bi-annual flower shows, Lalbagh houses India's largest collection of tropical plants. It is a peaceful escape in the heart of the city.

2. Bangalore Palace

A majestic palace built in Tudor-style architecture, inspired by Windsor Castle in England. It features elegant wood carvings and beautiful interiors.

3. Vidhana Soudha

The seat of the state legislature of Karnataka. It is an imposing building constructed in a style described as Neo-Dravidian.

4. Nandi Hills

An ancient hill fortress located just outside the city. It is famous for its breathtaking sunrise views and foggy mornings.

5. Cubbon Park

Often called the 'lungs' of Bangalore, this massive park is filled with greenery, statues, and historic buildings like the State Library.
